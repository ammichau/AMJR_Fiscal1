These file PSincCompute.m computes the share of income in a country held by the top X% of the population.
It requires as an input the parameters of the log-normal distribution calculated in
Pinkovskiy, Maxim and Xavier Sala-i-Martin (2009), “Parametric Estimations of the World Distribution of Income”, NBER Working Paper 15433.
